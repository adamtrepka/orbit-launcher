HOW TO PLAY

  1. Read the mission -- you'll get a target orbit type with specific altitude, inclination, and eccentricity to match.
  2. Set your launch parameters. In Arcade mode (default) you set just 2 sliders: direction and altitude. In Pro mode you get full control over all 5 parameters.
  3. Launch! Watch your rocket fly. A ghost trajectory preview (blue line) updates live as you change parameters.
  4. Score 0-100 based on orbit accuracy (60%) and fuel efficiency (40%).

GOAL

Match the target orbit (gold ring) by setting launch parameters. The rocket performs up to 3 burns: ascent, injection, and auto-circularization at apogee.

CONTROL MODES

Arcade Mode (default)
Two sliders: Direction and Target Altitude. The game auto-computes optimal elevation, thrust, and injection altitude for you. Focus on aim and altitude!
Pro Mode
Full control over all 5 parameters. Switch via the toggle at the top of the launch panel.

THE 3 BURN PHASES

1. Ascent
The rocket launches and thrusts for 300 seconds, using the thrust % of total fuel. After ascent, it coasts upward.
2. Injection Burn
When the rocket reaches injection altitude, it fires prograde (forward) to raise the orbit to the target apogee.
3. Auto-Circularization
At apogee, the rocket tries to circularize. The amount depends on thrust: ≤50% = full circ, ≥62% = no circ. The 50-62% range gives partial circ for elliptical orbits.

ALL 5 SLIDERS (Pro Mode)

Direction / Azimuth (0-360°)
Compass heading. 90°=East (equatorial orbits), 0°/180°=North/South (polar orbits). Controls inclination.
Elevation (15-85°)
Angle above the horizon. ~30-45° is typical. Higher = steeper initial climb.
Ascent Thrust (10-100%)
Dual purpose: sets ascent energy AND controls auto-circ. ≤50% → circular orbits. 55-62% → elliptical orbits. This is the KEY slider for eccentricity.
Injection Altitude (100-5,000 km)
Where the injection burn fires. Keep this LOW (~200-300 km) for most missions. The rocket must reach this altitude first.
Target Altitude / Apogee (150-50,000 km)
How high you want the orbit to go. The game auto-computes the injection delta-V needed.

QUICK TIPS

  • The blue ghost line shows your predicted trajectory. Match it to the gold target ring.
  • In Arcade mode, the inclination readout below the Direction slider helps you dial in the right angle.
  • For polar orbits: set direction near 0° or 350° (slightly west compensates for Earth rotation).
  • Switch to Pro mode for fine-tuned control over elliptical orbits (GTO, Molniya, Tundra).
  • Fuel budget: 16 km/s total. Scoring: 60% orbit accuracy + 40% fuel efficiency.
